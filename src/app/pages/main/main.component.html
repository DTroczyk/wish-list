Welcome to Wish List

What is Wish List?

Wish List is an application where you can add items you want as a gift. You can also buy gifts for family or friends. You can decide who sees your items. You can show them to your all friends or just some of them. However, remember that your friends can share with others. You don't know who wants to buy gift for you because it's a surprise!

You or other users can reserve an item for purchase for another user. This prevents the item from being duplicated. And if gift is too expensive, other users can buy it together. So, if you have decided that you want to buy gift for your friend, you have to reserved it.

For Who?

This app is for people who know what are they need and don't know what wheir friend needs. If they use this app, their problem can be resolved.

Remember, adding a wish does not guarantee that it will be fulfilled. The app allows your friends to see what you want. Fulfillment depends on whether one of them will want to fulfill it.
